About Chomee

Chomee is a software platform dedicated to educating to children in rural Africa. We accomplish this through students downloading lessons directly onto their mobile phones in an area with internet, then being able to complete the lesson offline where they do not have access to internet. We are currently partnered with a high school in Zimbabwe where we set up a Starlink internet connection.
